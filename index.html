GSSC Digital Decoding Apps

Swift/SwiftUI, Objective-C, Keras, OpenCV, RESTAPI, JSON, OAuth

While working at GSSC, I was responsible for redeveloping dozens of iOS applications. Each application utilizes OpenCV image processing for real-time augmented reality steganography decoding, with the addition of binary classification algorithms to classify the scanned product as authentic or counterfeit.
